What is this project?

CAR PARKING WEBSITE is a website and my personal project to showcase some of the less skilled drivers I've seen, mostly in and around KlaipÄ—da. In 2018 my roomate and I made the observation that our parking lot's guidelines are appearently invisable to some drivers. After seeing that the poor parking jobs occoured really quite frequently we took note and pointed it out when exceptionally bad parking jobs were visable outside our doorm window.
